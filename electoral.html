Electoral Bond Data Search App

The Electoral Bond Data Search App aims to provide users with a convenient way to explore and analyze electoral bond data. Electoral bonds are financial instruments that enable individuals and corporations to donate funds to political parties in India. The Electoral Bond Data Search App aims to empower users, including journalists, researchers, policymakers, and the general public, to access, analyze, and understand electoral bond transactions more effectively, thereby promoting transparency and accountability in political financing.
